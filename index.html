    How to Make a WhatsApp Sticker

    1. Upload a photo. Drag and drop a JPG, PNG, or WebP image into the upload box above, or click to browse your files.
    2. Customize it. The tool automatically crops your photo into a 512×512px square. Add one or more text layers, pick a font, color, and position, and toggle an outline for readability.
    3. Download. Click "Download sticker.webp" — the tool compresses your image to WebP format under 100KB, which is WhatsApp's sticker size requirement.
    4. Add it to WhatsApp. Use a sticker-pack app such as Sticker Maker or Sticker.ly to import your downloaded WebP file into WhatsApp as a custom sticker pack.

    WhatsApp Sticker Size Requirements

    WhatsApp stickers must be square images sized exactly 512×512 pixels, saved in WebP format, and kept under 100KB per sticker. This tool handles all three requirements automatically: it crops your photo to a perfect square, exports it as WebP, and compresses the file until it fits comfortably under the 100KB limit.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What image formats can I upload?

    Is there a file size limit for my sticker?

    WhatsApp requires each sticker to be under 100KB. This tool compresses your exported WebP automatically to stay under that limit, and shows a warning if an extremely detailed image can't be compressed enough.

    Do I need to sign up or install an app?

    No. This is a free, browser-based tool — there's no account, no signup form, and nothing to install. Just upload, customize, and download.

    Will my sticker have a watermark?
