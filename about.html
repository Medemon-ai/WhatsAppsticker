About StickerSnap

A Simple, Private Way to Turn Photos Into WhatsApp Stickers

StickerSnap was built to make sticker creation fast, easy, and worry-free — no accounts, no watermarks, and no photos uploaded anywhere but your own device.

🔒 100% private 🚫 No signup ✨ No watermark ⚡ Instant results

Our Mission

Making a WhatsApp sticker shouldn't require installing an app, creating an account, or handing your photos over to a stranger's server. StickerSnap exists to make sticker creation as simple as it should always have been: open a page, upload a photo, and download your sticker — all inside your own browser.

Why Choose Us

1 Built for Speed

Every photo is processed locally using your browser's Canvas API. There's no upload wait, no server queue — your sticker preview updates instantly as you type and customize.

2 Easy for Anyone

Three simple steps — upload, customize, download. No tutorials needed, no confusing settings. Works great on both desktop and mobile.

3 Privacy First

We don't want your photos, and we don't ask for them. StickerSnap processes images entirely on your device, so nothing is ever transmitted to a server or stored anywhere.

Features

  • Automatic 512×512 cropping — every sticker is sized exactly to WhatsApp's requirement.
  • Custom text layers — add multiple captions with different fonts, colors, and positions.
  • Smart compression — exports as WebP, automatically kept under WhatsApp's 100KB sticker limit.
  • Mobile friendly — the entire tool is fully responsive and works from a phone browser.
  • No account required — nothing to sign up for, nothing to remember.
  • Completely free — no premium tier, no hidden paywall, no watermark.

Privacy First

Your photos never leave your device. StickerSnap doesn't upload images to any server, doesn't use tracking scripts to profile your activity, and doesn't store your files anywhere after you close the tab. Everything — cropping, text, color, compression — happens locally in your browser.

Looking Ahead

StickerSnap is under active development. Planned improvements include more font and sticker-shape options, batch sticker creation, and direct sticker-pack export — all while keeping the same commitment to speed, simplicity, and privacy.
